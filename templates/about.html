About Yonqui

Yoruba Nation Quintessentials (Yonqui)

Yonqui is a vibrant, forward-thinking community dedicated to the empowerment, unity, and global advancement of the Yoruba people. Our mission is to foster cultural pride, professional excellence, and social impact among Yoruba descendants worldwide.

Our Vision

To be the leading network for Yoruba professionals, creatives, and advocates, championing heritage, innovation, and positive change.

Our Mission
  • Connect Yoruba individuals and organizations globally
  • Promote Yoruba culture, language, and values
  • Support professional growth, mentorship, and entrepreneurship
  • Organize events, workshops, and cultural festivals
  • Advocate for social justice, education, and economic empowerment
Why Join Yonqui?
  • Exclusive access to resources and opportunities
  • Networking with like-minded Yoruba professionals
  • Mentorship and career support
  • Celebrate and preserve Yoruba heritage
  • Contribute to impactful community projects
Who Can Join?
  • Yoruba professionals, students, and creatives
  • Organizations and businesses with Yoruba interests
  • Allies and supporters of Yoruba advancement
